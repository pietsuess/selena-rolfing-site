What is Rolfing?

Rolfing™ is a method of restructuring the body through movement and touch. By learning to let go of unconscious holding patterns and postural habits, many chronic conditions are cured and replaced with a feeling of fluidity and lightness.

Who is Rolfing good for?

Rolfing™ benefits anyone who wants to restore mobility and healthy functioning to joints and connective tissues. Rolfing can reduce and eliminate chronic pain and restricted movement caused by:

Poor posture & bad habits Childbirth Chronic back pain Shoulder, knee or hip pain Repetitive stress injury Plantar fasciitis Depression Scoliosis & Sciatica Whiplash accidents Carpal tunnel syndrome TMJ ‘lock jaw’ syndrome Bunions

What is structural integration?

A dynamic touch therapy that works with the fascia (a web of connective tissue under the skin) to systematically release, realign and balance the whole body, thereby resolving chronic pain from habitual postural compensations.
