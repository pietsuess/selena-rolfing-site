What kind of shoes are best for me?

By Selena La Brooy • March 26, 2020

Many people ask about footwear options. The best shoes offer a wide toe box and maximum mobility for toe and arch movement. Minimal support allows you to strengthen foot muscles rather than rely on built-in arch support. Rigid soles and arch support actually immobilize your feet and weaken intrinsic foot muscles. Good foot health maintains mobility and resilience.

For deeper understanding, Selena recommends biomechanist Katy Bowman’s book Whole Body Barefoot, which explains why minimal shoes work and how to transition safely. Bowman emphasizes: “we cannot go from high support to barefoot shoes or minimal support overnight and not expect injury.” Gradual strengthening is essential.

Recommended Practices

  • Use toe spreaders 20 minutes twice daily while seated or lying down
  • Build foot strength progressively before switching to minimal shoes
  • Visit nutritiousmovement.com for additional resources
